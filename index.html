Pablo Díaz is a versatile professional with a diverse background spanning music composition, sound art, and craftsmanship in ceramics and glass. He has participated in numerous exhibitions and conferences internationally, showcasing his works that blend artistic vision with scientific knowledge.

Outside of his artistic endeavors, Pablo is fluent in multiple languages and has certifications in various fields related to his interests.
