About

Pablo Díaz is a versatile professional in music composition, sound art, ceramics, and glass craftsmanship. With a Master's degree in Science and Art of Glass and Ceramics from NOVA School of Science and Technology, and a Master's in Sound Art from Universitat de Barcelona, Pablo combines technical expertise with creative skills.
