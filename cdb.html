ADB|CDB
| CLAY |


ADB|CDB (Asuciación de Barro, Asuciació de Barri, Asujação de Barro, Club de amigos del Barro) is an instance of exploration, interaction, and recognition. It first emerged in 2019 as part of the research project “Enfoques y experiencias temporales y físico-sonoras con arcilla. Proyecto interdisciplinario sobre el trabajo sonoro desde y con la materia” in the sound art master's program at the University of Barcelona. The first action of the Clay Association involves the creation of a social network to share content related to clay and soil as an open source to everyone. Over the years, the Association has been activated for various purposes, such as workshops, performances, explorations, and installations. The Association operates collaboratively with other artists who interact with it as it exists. The Association "does not have a fixed character. It aspires to be an autonomous zone of free research and expression, ductile and resilient in its dimensions, eager to grow, expand, diffuse, destroy itself, and re-emerge if necessary." Through different means, both analog and digital, and people who gather around them, the Association takes shape and is activated.
