The_PBO
| GLASS |


The_PBO explores glass and its behavior in terms of sound. The nature of glass, likely as the objects obtained from its interaction with humans, is full of details. Those details can be perceived directly or subtly as sound and sonic features. The_PBO interacts with glass and its crafts, such as glass blowing, lampworking, and glass fusing, experiencing sound phenomena to an extent that might lead to a technical approach, a performative action, or a register understood as witnessing a particular phenomenon. The output tends to be related to hybrid catalogs, diaries, and performative research such as live actions, ASMR moments (online or not), or workshops.
